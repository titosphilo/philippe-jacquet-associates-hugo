Is Psychotherapy Marbella confidential and regulated by local laws ?

Psychotherapy Marbella services operate under strict confidentiality and legal regulations. In Spain, psychotherapy is governed by both national health laws and European privacy regulations, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Therapists in Marbella must be registered professionals, often licensed as psychologists or psychiatrists under Spanish law.
