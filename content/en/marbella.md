# Therapy & Coaching in Marbella

English-speaking psychotherapy and executive coaching on the Costa del Sol. The Marbella practice serves the significant English-speaking community in southern Spain — British and international residents, those who spend extended periods on the coast, and professionals based in the area who need clinical or coaching support that goes beyond what is locally available.

The work available in Marbella draws on the same clinical depth as the London practices. Integrative and Jungian psychotherapy, executive coaching informed by ESSEC Business School training, and group workshops for personal and professional development.

## Who Comes to Marbella

The Marbella practice serves British and international expats, retirees navigating significant life transitions, professionals based in southern Spain, and visitors who use extended stays to begin or continue therapeutic work. It also serves people who find that the pace and environment of the Costa del Sol is itself useful to the process — that being away from ordinary life creates space for the kind of reflection that is harder to access at home.

## Executive Coaching in Marbella

Executive coaching in Marbella draws on the same dual background as the London offering: ESSEC Business School training \(ranked among Europe’s top ten by the Financial Times\) and 25 years of clinical practice as an integrative psychotherapist and Jungian analyst. For leaders and senior professionals who find themselves in Marbella with time to think, this is an unusual and valuable combination.
