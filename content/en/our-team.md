# Our Team

# Our Team

The practice is led by Dr Jacquet, with a team of associates whose qualifications and specialisms cover the full range of presentations seen at the practice. All clinicians hold recognised professional qualifications and maintain current registration with the relevant professional bodies — UKCP, BACP, EMDR Association, or equivalent.

## Dr Jacquet — Principal Clinician

Dr Jacquet is an integrative psychotherapist, Jungian analyst, EMDR practitioner with over 20 years of experience, trained art psychotherapist, addiction specialist, and executive coach. He holds a Doctorate of Professional Studies and is the only person in Europe to have received a doctorate specifically on male eating disorders. He trained at ESSEC Business School for executive coaching and at Hazelden and Hope-One for addiction specialism. He has 25 years of clinical practice across London, Brussels, Monaco, Paris, Dubai, Nairobi, and online.

## Associates

Associates at the practice are experienced clinicians across psychotherapy, counselling, EMDR, art therapy, and coaching. Referrals to associates are made based on clinical fit, specialism, and availability. All associate work is supervised within the practice framework.

For enquiries about a specific clinician or for information about who might be most appropriate for a particular presenting issue, the best starting point is the initial consultation. The practice will match clients to the clinician whose background and approach is most suited to their specific needs.
