# Therapy in Colchester, Essex

The Colchester practice brings specialist integrative and Jungian psychotherapy to Essex. For those living and working in Colchester, Chelmsford, Ipswich and the surrounding area, it offers clinical work of the same depth and quality as the London practices — without the need to travel to the city.

Dr Jacquet has maintained a presence in Colchester for many years, developing a practice that serves the particular needs of the Essex community. The work here draws on the same clinical breadth: individual psychotherapy, Jungian analysis, EMDR for trauma, eating disorder treatment, addiction counselling, and clinical supervision for other therapists.

## What Is Available

Individual psychotherapy and Jungian analytic work for the full range of adult presentations. EMDR for trauma and PTSD. Eating disorder treatment, including male eating disorders in which Philippe holds Europe's only doctoral research. Addiction counselling. Bereavement and grief work. Stress and anxiety therapy. Clinical supervision for qualified therapists and counsellors in Essex.

## A Note on Supervision

The Colchester practice is one of the few in Essex to offer clinical supervision at doctoral level. For qualified therapists seeking supervision that goes beyond managerial oversight into genuine depth engagement with clinical material, this practice offers something that is rarely available outside London.
