Harley Street has been synonymous with private medicine and specialist psychological care in London for over a century. Dr Jacquet's practice has been located here since 2002. This is where the main practice is based - in discreet, accessible premises, just steps from Regent's Park.

The practice offers integrative psychotherapy and Jungian analysis, EMDR therapy, clinical hypnotherapy, art therapy, and executive coaching. Consultations are conducted in English and French.

## A Specialist Practice

Dr Jacquet is an integrative psychotherapist and Jungian analyst with over 25 years of clinical experience in London and internationally. He is an EMDR practitioner with over 20 years' experience, a qualified art therapist, and a specialist in addictions trained at Hazelden Foundation and Hope-One. He holds a Doctorate of Professional Practice - the only practitioner in Europe with doctoral research specifically on male eating disorders. He trained in executive coaching at ESSEC, ranked among the top 10 European business schools by the Financial Times.
